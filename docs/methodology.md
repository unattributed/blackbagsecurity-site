      <h1>Methodology</h1>
      <p>Every engagement follows a strict operating model with explicit scope control, safety rails, and evidence capture.</p>
    </div>

    <div class="split">
      <div class="stack reveal" data-reveal style="--delay: 0.05s;">
        <h3>Engagement lifecycle</h3>
        <ol class="steps">
          <li><span>01 Align</span>Define objectives, access paths, and stop conditions.</li>
          <li><span>02 Operate</span>Execute tradecraft with safety rails and logging.</li>
          <li><span>03 Report</span>Deliver reproduction steps, impact, and fix guidance.</li>
          <li><span>04 Retest</span>Validate closures and document closure statements.</li>
        </ol>
      </div>
      <div class="stack reveal" data-reveal style="--delay: 0.1s;">
        <h3>Scope control</h3>
        <ul class="outcomes">
          <li>Rules of engagement with escalation paths and stop conditions.</li>
          <li>Change windows and operational impact constraints.</li>
          <li>Defined data handling and retention expectations.</li>
        </ul>
      </div>
    </div>
  </div>
</section>
